Cyberia Camp is an annual event organized by NUSSU Committee of Information &Technology (commIT). The aim of this 3-day camp is to impart valuable IT skills (e.g. graphic design & editing) to the participants in an interactive and informative camp setting.

Cyberia Camp has been our highlight for 10 years with much success, owing to the overwhelming interest from the NUS population and favorable feedbacks from the participants.
